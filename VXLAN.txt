VXLAN is the data plane of SDA since it can carry encapsulated L2 information.
VXLAN Header is 8 bytes: VNI ID is 24 bit and SGT is 4 bit
VNID is 24 bits
VXLAN Destination port 4789
in SDA VXLAN is used as Layer 2 tunneling

VTEP is FEN
VXLAN group policy option(VXLAN-GPO)
VXLAN Scalabe group tag which is managed by cisco trustsec
LISP is control-plane
